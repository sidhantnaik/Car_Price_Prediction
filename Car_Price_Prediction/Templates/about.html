Project Mission

My project aims to provide accurate and reliable car price predictions to assist buyers and sellers in making informed decisions. By leveraging advanced algorithms and data analysis, I strive to ensure that the predictions are as precise as possible.

About Me

I am a passionate student specializing in data science and machine learning. This project is a culmination of my learning and practical application of these concepts.

Machine Learning Model

The car price prediction model is built using the Random Forest Regressor algorithm. This ensemble learning method is effective for regression tasks and enhances prediction accuracy by combining multiple decision trees.

Accuracy Model

The model was trained on a diverse dataset containing various features related to car specifications, market trends, and historical prices. After extensive training and validation, the model achieved an impressive accuracy of 93.03%, making it a reliable tool for predicting car prices.
